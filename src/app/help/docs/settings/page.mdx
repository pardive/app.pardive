# Settings

Configure how your workspace behaves across regions, language defaults, domains, and member access.

## What you can configure
- **Workspace** – region, locale, domains, and member access
- **Appearance** – theme, logo, brand colors
- **Sidebar** – navigation items and group order
- **Branding** – assets and identity used across products

> Tip: All settings are scoped to your **current workspace** unless noted otherwise.
